Andrea Lynn♥

Born and raised in Austin TX

As a young girl Ive always known I wanted to become a hairstylist. Being blessed with my mother doing hair, the salon become my second home. By the time I was 16 I started beauty school at Paul Mitchell. I graduated beauty school before I graduated high school. The day of my high school graduation I got a call from Toni & Guy saying I got the job! I specialized in cut and color for over 5 years, and had an amazing opportunity to be an educator for over 3 year. Fueling my passion in education I decided to enroll in The Academy of Hair Design. Now Ive become my own boss sharing a studio at The Salons @ University Oaks. Following my goals in working in weddings, fashion shows, and photoshoots! I love the freedom of being creative in any way possible!
